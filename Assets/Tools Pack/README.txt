Tool Pack v1.0
by Stanley3D
---------

This is a free package of two models (a shovel and an axe), two materials and two texture sets. 
Animations and sounds are not included. 

Textures are 4K PBR (Diffuse, Metallic, Normal, Ambient Occlusion)
Models are rigged.
You can find source blend files in Source.zip

Please, check my other works: http://u3d.as/Kx6

How to use:
-----------
Drop a prefab in your scene. Enjoy!
